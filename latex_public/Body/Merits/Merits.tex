\subsection{\mrl{} has sustained national and international acclaim in his field of expertise}

\label{subsec:SustainedAcclaim}



\mrl{} has not only achieved a national acclaim but has sustained this recognition over a continuous period, evidenced by a consistent trajectory of awards, media appearances, and expert roles.



His recognition by the Head of State and municipal leaders has been sustained over multiple years. \mrl{} received an Honorary Diploma from the Mayor of Astana in 2022 and 2024. He is a recurring subject of interest for major media outlets. The media consistently seeks \mrl{}'s expertise to explain complex digital trends to the public. His media presence includes different interviews, such as a distinguished alumnus for L.N.~Gumilyov ENU, a technical discussion on "Smart City'' technologies for \emph{HubTalks}, and a live broadcast interview on iKOMEK operations for the TV program \emph{By Trends} (\cref{sec:media}).



\mrl{} is continuously invited as an expert to guide the next generation of specialists. Demonstrating his sustained authority, he is frequently invited to evaluate professional competitions.
