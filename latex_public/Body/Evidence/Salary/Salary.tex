\subsection{Evidence that ~\mrl\ has commanded a high salary relative to others in the field}

\label{subsec:Salary}



The following section lists independent salary surveys for Astana, Republic of Kazakhstan, for the role IT Project Manager. These sources serve as benchmarks for comparison against Mr. Taximov's compensation.



The following independent sources establish the baseline market salary for an IT Project Manager in Astana.



\underline{HR Praktika (Formal Salary Certificate)}

HR Praktika issued a signed certificate defining job duties and qualifications using an averaging method across multiple surveys (People Consulting, EY, Habr, and hh.ru).

\begin{itemize}[nosep]

    \item Average Monthly Salary: 500 000 KZT

    \item Annualized Total: 6 000 000 KZT

\end{itemize}[nosep]

\ExhibitRef{HRPraktikaPMFinal}



\underline{HeadHunter.kz (Official Letter)}

A signed letter from HeadHunder Kazakhstan was received. It shows the 2024 mean monthly salary for Astana based on vacancies listed on their platform.

\begin{itemize}[nosep]

    \item Mean Monthly Salary: 442 000 KZT

    \item Annualized Total: 5 304 000 KZT

\end{itemize}[nosep]

\ExhibitRef{HHPM2024Letter}



\underline{RecCom Recruiting (Market Overview)}

RecCom provided a 2024 salary overview for Astana, signed and sealed by the Executive Director. It includes role definitions and methodology summaries.

\begin{itemize}[nosep]

    \item Monthly Salary: 558 000 KZT

    \item Annualized Total: 6 696 000 KZT

\end{itemize}[nosep]

\ExhibitRef{RecComAstana2024Salaries}



\underline{Bureau of National Statistics (Official Government Letter)}

The Department of the Bureau of National Statistics for Astana issued official response No.~ЖТ 2025 03546738 (dated 13~October~2025). The data is derived from the nationwide \emph{“Report on the structure and distribution of wages”} (Index 2 T wages) and follows the National Classifier of Occupations (НКЗ).

\begin{itemize}[nosep]

    \item Nominal Average Monthly: 758 791 KZT

    \item Annualized Total: 9 105 492 KZT

\end{itemize}[nosep]

\ExhibitRef{BNSAstanaICTPM2024Letter}
