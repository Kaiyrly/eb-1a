\subsection{Evidence that ~\mrl\ has commanded a high salary relative to others in the field}

\label{subsec:Salary}

~\mrl\ works in IT Project Management. The following section lists independent compensation surveys for \textbf{Astana, Republic of Kazakhstan}. They cover the role of \emph{IT Project Manager}. These sources serve as benchmarks for comparison.

\subsubsection{2024 market compensation for Astana from independent sources}

\textbf{HR Praktika formal salary certificate covering methodology and median.}

HR Praktika issued a signed certificate. It defines job duties and qualifications. The document describes the averaging method across multiple surveys. It presents the \textbf{2024 final average monthly salary} for Astana.

\begin{itemize}

\item \textbf{IT Project Manager}

\begin{itemize}

\item People Consulting shows 485 000 KZT. EY shows 510 000 KZT. Habr lists 450 000 KZT. The hh.ru sample for Astana shows 515 000 KZT. The minimum is 386 000 and the maximum is 643 000.

\item \emph{The final average monthly salary} is \textbf{500 000 KZT}.

\item \emph{The annualized total is} $500 000 \times 12 = \mathbf{6 000 000}$ KZT.

\end{itemize}

\end{itemize}

\textbf{HeadHunter.kz official letter showing mean salary.}

HeadHunter Kazakhstan released a signed letter. It shows the 2024 mean monthly salary for Astana. The figures rely on vacancies from hh.kz:

\begin{itemize}

\item \textbf{An IT Project Manager earns 442 000 KZT monthly.}

\end{itemize}

\noindent

\emph{The annual total is}

$$ 442 000 \times 12 = \mathbf{5 304 000}\ \text{KZT.} $$

\textbf{RecCom Recruiting market overview letter.}

RecCom provided a 2024 salary overview for Astana. It includes role definitions and a summary of methods.

\begin{itemize}

\item \textbf{An IT Project Manager earns 558 000 KZT per month. This totals $558 000 \times 12 = \mathbf{6 696 000}$ KZT annually.}

\end{itemize}

The overview explains data sources. The Executive Director signed and sealed the document.

\textbf{Bureau of National Statistics Astana Department official government letter covering methodology and average.}

The \emph{Department of the Bureau of National Statistics Agency for Strategic Planning and Reforms for the city of Astana} issued an official response. Letter No.~ЖТ 2025 03546738 is dated 13~October~2025. Head N.~Kozhakov signed it. The letter uses the \emph{Methodology for calculating average wages of employees}. Order No.~20 of 17~November~2023 approved this method. The data comes from the nationwide \emph{“Report on the structure and distribution of wages”} index \textbf{2 T wages}. Occupational mapping follows the \emph{National Classifier of Occupations of the Republic of Kazakhstan НКЗ}. Order No.~130 од of 11~May~2017 established these codes.

\begin{itemize}

\item \textbf{IT Project Manager statistics show $\mathbf{758\,791}$ KZT per month as the nominal average.}

\item \emph{The total over 12 months is} $758\,791 \times 12 = \mathbf{9\,105\,492}$ KZT.

\end{itemize}

A signed \emph{Salary Statement} from iKOMEK109 confirms \mrl’s position as \textbf{IT Project Manager}. The statement shows the following details:

\begin{itemize}

\item Total earnings in 2024 were 36 280 907.9 KZT.

\item The document lists pension contributions and taxes. The comparison below uses the gross value.

\end{itemize}

$\mathbf{468.99}$ KZT per USD

$$ \textbf{USD equivalent for context only is}\quad \frac{36 280 907.9\ \text{KZT}}{468.99\ \text{KZT/USD}} \approx \mathbf{77 360}\ \text{USD.} $$

The NBK is Kazakhstan’s central bank. It publishes daily USD to KZT rates. The average above is the simple mean of all daily fixes for 2024.
