\subsubsection{Letter of Appreciation from the President of the Republic of Kazakhstan (2024)}
\label{subsubsec:AwardPresident2024}

\SubSubSubSection{Context}

\mrl received an official \emph{Алғыс} (Letter of Appreciation) from the President of the Republic of Kazakhstan for his contribution to a large international forum hosted in Astana.

\SubSubSubSection{Proof of receipt}

The original presidential certificate bearing the President’s name, seal and signature is attached \ExhibitRef{PresAlghys2024}.

\SubSubSubSection{Why this qualifies under USCIS Criterion 1}

This is a national-level state recognition issued by the head of state. By its nature, a presidential letter of appreciation acknowledges excellence and notable contributions with nationwide recognition. The document itself specifies that the award is presented for significant assistance in organizing and conducting a high-level international event in Astana, i.e., excellence in the beneficiary’s professional field of digital transformation and information technologies.

\SubSubSubSection{No undue limits on competitors}

Presidential appreciations are conferred based on merit and contributions, not limited to a specific employer, locality or academic unit; therefore they are nationally recognized and open to qualified contributors across the country.
