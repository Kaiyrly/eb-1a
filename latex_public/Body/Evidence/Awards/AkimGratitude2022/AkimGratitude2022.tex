\subsubsection{Mayor of Astana — Letter of Appreciation (2022)}
\label{subsubsec:AwardAkimGratitude2022}

\SubSubSubSection{Context}

\mrl received a formal \emph{Алғыс хаты} from the Mayor of Astana, acknowledging his professional contribution to the city’s development in information technologies and digitalization.

\SubSubSubSection{Proof of receipt}

The signed and sealed letter from the Mayor of Astana is attached \ExhibitRef{AkimAlgys2022}.

\SubSubSubSection{Why this qualifies}

Municipal-level awards from the capital’s administration are widely recognized in the field and are conferred for excellence and meaningful civic and professional contributions. They are not internal employer awards but issued by the city’s executive authority, which serves the broader public.

\SubSubSubSection{Breadth of competition}

Such recognitions are available to professionals across organizations who contributed substantially to city-level initiatives.
