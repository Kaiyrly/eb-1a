\subsubsection{Mayor of Astana — Certificate of Honor (Құрмет грамотасы) (2022)}
\label{subsubsec:AwardAkimKurmet2022}

\SubSubSubSection{Context}

In 2022, \mrl was awarded the \emph{Құрмет грамотасы} (Certificate of Honor) by the Mayor of Astana for demonstrated professionalism and leadership in organizing and conducting the presidential election processes and related digital initiatives.

\SubSubSubSection{Proof of receipt}

The original certificate signed by the Mayor is attached \ExhibitRef{AkimKurmet2022}.

\SubSubSubSection{Why this qualifies}

A Certificate of Honor is a high municipal distinction recognizing excellence and meritorious service; it is publicly conferred by the capital’s administration and widely recognized in Kazakhstan.

\SubSubSubSection{Open and competitive nature}

These honors are awarded based on merit and citywide impact, without limitation to a single employer or school.
