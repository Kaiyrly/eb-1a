\subsubsection{Presidential Letter of Appreciation (2022)}
\label{subsubsec:AwardPresident2022}

\SubSubSubSection{Context}

In 2022, \mrl was awarded a presidential \emph{Алғыс хаты} for his contribution to the preparation and conduct of the national presidential election process.

\SubSubSubSection{Proof of receipt}

The certificate with state seal and signature confirms the award \ExhibitRef{PresAlghys2022}.

\SubSubSubSection{Why this qualifies}

This is a national award issued by the highest state authority, recognizing excellence and trusted service in a matter of national importance. Such awards are nationally recognized and attest to the recipient’s distinguished contribution and reputation.

\SubSubSubSection{Open competition}

Eligibility is not restricted to members of a single employer or locality; awards are granted based on performance and impact.
