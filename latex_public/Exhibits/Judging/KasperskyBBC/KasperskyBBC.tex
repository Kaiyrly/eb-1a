\paragraph{Exhibit \texttt{KasperskyBBC} — BBC News: Kaspersky exposes state-level “Flame” cyber-attack.}
\begin{itemize}[nosep]\small
  \item \textbf{What this shows.} BBC News reports that Kaspersky Lab identified the “Flame” malware and conducted research “in conjunction with the UN’s International Telecommunication Union,” calling Flame “one of the most complex threats ever discovered.” It notes “more than 600 specific targets,” spanning individuals, businesses, academic institutions and government systems.
  \item \textbf{Why this matters (distinguished source).} BBC is an international outlet; the article positions Kaspersky as a primary investigator working with a UN body, evidencing the company’s global reputation and technical leadership in cybersecurity.
\end{itemize}[nosep]
