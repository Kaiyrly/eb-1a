\subsubsection{iKOMEK109 — Presentation slides (operating model \& roadmap)}
\label{subsubsec:IKomekPresentation}

\paragraph{Document identity.}
A slide deck used to brief the Mayor’s office and city stakeholders on the iKOMEK 2.0 operating model: governance cadence, KPI dashboards, contact-center workflows, Situation Center pipeline, enforcement support and digital channel expansion.

\paragraph{What the slides show (overview).}
\begin{itemize}[nosep]\small
  \item \textbf{Mission \& structure:} the integrated 24/7 contact center, Situation Center and Analytics Unit.
  \item \textbf{Operating cadence:} weekly executive reporting that drove a \emph{76\%} reduction in unresolved urgent issues (112$\rightarrow$27).
  \item \textbf{Process \& KPIs:} backlog liquidation and a \emph{28.4\%} YoY increase in closures (\emph{426{,}542} in 2023).
  \item \textbf{Channels \& UX:} mobile app and Telegram bot increasing online submissions by \emph{22\%}.
  \item \textbf{Public safety chain:} how observation $\rightarrow$ analytics $\rightarrow$ field action supported \emph{23} criminal cases and \emph{19{,}384} offenses for \emph{KZT 52{,}008{,}750} in fines.
  \item \textbf{Data engineering:} voter-list integrity pipeline (Python/Pandas/NumPy) achieving \emph{100\%} accuracy.
  \item \textbf{Roadmap:} the CRM core, external integrations, executive rating tools, DB optimization, unified SSO channels.
\end{itemize}[nosep]

\paragraph{Relevance to USCIS Criterion 8.}
The deck visualizes Mr.\ Taximov’s \textbf{leading} role (organizational design, roadmap, dashboards) and \textbf{critical} impact (quantified outcomes across safety, service and elections) within a \textbf{distinguished} municipal enterprise.
